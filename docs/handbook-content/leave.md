# Company Policies

This section contains leave policies.

# Marriage Leave
5 Working days

# Elder/Family Care Leave
Up to 2 working days

# Maternity Leave
16 Weeks (Subject to MOM requirements)

# Paternity Leave
4 Weeks (Subject to MOM requirements)

# Shared Parental Leave
Up to 6 weeks (Subject to MOM requirements)

# Childcare Leave
Up to 6 working days annually (Subject to MOM requirements)

# Extended Childcare Leave
2 working days annually (Subject to MOM Requirements)

# No Pay Leave
Up to 10 consecutive calendar days (Subject to management approval)

# Long-Term Sick Leave
Applicable to confirmed employees
- First 4 months: Full Pay
- Second 4 months: Half Pay
- Third 4 months: No Pay

# Company Time-Off
- New Year's Eve
- Lunar New Year's Eve
- Christmas Eve

# Public Holidays
LGESL observes all Singapore Gazetted public Holidays
