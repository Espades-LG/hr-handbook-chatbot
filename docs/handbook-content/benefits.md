# Company Policies

This section contains all company benefits.

# Group Insurance Coverage
Coverage includes: 
- Hospital & Surgical
- Outpatient Clinical
- Outpatient Specialist
- Dental
- Personal Accident Insurance

# Health Screening
Eligible Employees:
- Permanent employees
- Contract employees (excluding promoters)
- Minimum 1 year service
- Frequency: Once every 2 years (odd-numbered years).

# Birthday Gift
Confirmed employees are eligible for SGD 50 vouchers on their birthday month.

# Matrimonial Gift
Confirmed employees are eligible for SGD 100 vouchers upon marriage

# Newborn Gift
Confirmed employees are eligible for SGD 100 vouchers upon childbirth

# Get-Well Gift
Hospitalized confirmed employee may receive a get-well hamper

# Bereavement Support
Floral wreath valued at SGD 100 for qualifying family members

# Employee Referral Program
Referral Fee of SGD 250, payable upon successful confirmation of referred empployee.

# Winter Clothing Reimbursement
- Up to SGD 250
- Business travel destinations with average temperature ≤ 15°C.
- Claimable once every 5 years.

# Long Service Awards
- 5 years - SGD 500
- 10 years - SGD 1,000
- 15 years - SGD 1,500
- 20 years - SGD 2,000
- 25 years - SGD 2,500
- 30+ years - SGD 3,000

# Staff Purchase Program
- Available to all employees, yearly contract staff, and promoters.
- Subject to staff purchase policy
