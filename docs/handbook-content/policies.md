# Company Policies

Last Updated: 2026-07-06
Owner: HR Department

# Employment Policy
- Full-time permanent employees work at least 40 hours per week.
- Employment is subjected to:
- Medical fitness assessment
- No conflicting third-party obligations.
- Valid work permit (for non-Singaporean)
- Employment status changes due to promotion or transfer will be communicated by HR

# Attendance & Punctuality Policy
- Employees must maintain punctuality and attendance
- Unauthorized leave may result in disciplinary action.
- AWOL for 3 consecutive days is deemed voluntary resignation and employment may be terminated.

# Working Hours Policy
- Suntec Office: Monday - Friday: 9:00AM - 6:00PM
- Service Centre: Monday - Friday: 8:30AM - 6:15PM
- Saturday: According to duty Roster

# Probation & Confirmation Policy
- Employees serve probation as stated in appointment letter
- Company may extend probation if deem necessary.
- Confirmation depends on satisfactory performance review
- No salary adjustment upon confirmation.

# Dress Code Policy
- Smart Casual
- Acceptable (Business Shirts, Blouses, Polo Shirts, Jeans, Sneakers)
- Unacceptable (Tank tops, Singlets, Shorts, Mini-skirts, Flip flops, Crocs)
  
# Confidentiality Policy
- Employee must protect: Compensation information, Financial information, Product Plans, Trade Secrets, Business Strategies, Customer information.
- Violation may lead to discipinary action or termination.

# Gifts & Donations Policy
- Employees must not solicit or accept gifts from parties dealing with LG
- Gift received must be declared if refusal is impractical.
- Personal gifts not related to company business are allowed.

# Business Travel Policy
- Prior approval required via EP
- Management approval required at least 1 month before travel.
- IBTS Submission required at least 2 weeks before travel.
- Expenses must be supported with receipts.
- Economy class is standard travel class.

# Flexible Work Arrangement (FWA) Policy
- Employees may request remote work by HR-portal
- Manager approval is mandatory
- Non-compliance or drop in work performance may result in withdrawal of FWA privileges.

# Employee Grievance Policy
- Employees should first approach their supervisor 
- Formal grievances may be submitted to HR if it about their supervisor.
- Confidentiality and non-retaliation are assured.

# Workplace Harassment Policy
- Harassment is prohibited
- It includes offensive behaviour related but not limited to: Race, Gender, Religion, Disability, Sexual orientation.
- Complaints are investigated confidentially.
