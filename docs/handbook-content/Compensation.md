# Company Policies

This section contains all compensation policies.

# Salary Payment

- Monthly Salary paid on the 25th of every month
- Unless if it falls on a weekend, then the earliest working day before.

# Salary Review

Conducted annually in January/February, Effective date: 1st March
Employee must be:
- Join by 1st October the year before
- Be confirmed by 31st December

# Annual Wage Supplement (AWS)
- Paid in December
- Equivalent to 13th Month Salary.
- Prorated for eligible employees joined during the eyar.

# Short- Term Incentive (STI)
Paid Annually in March
Eligible:
- Confirmed Support
Non Eligible:
- Probation employees
- Employees who resigned before payout
- Employees under other commission schemes

# CPF Contributions
- Applicable to Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents
- CPF dedeuctions follow statutory requirements.

# Overtime Compensation
- EA-covered employees : Based on Government Regulations
- Service Centre Non-managers: SGD 10/hour
- Others: Off-in-lieu

# Meal reimbursement
Up to SGD 10
Eligible for overtime beyond 8:30 PM

# Taxi Claims
Eligible for:
- Overtime beyond 8:30 PM
- Early work before 7:30 PM
- Weekend/ Public Holiday duties

# Transport Allowance
- For employees requiring extensive local travel, paid monthly through payroll.

# Mileage Claim
- SGD 0.50 per Kilometer
- Applicable if employee uses personal vehicle for business
